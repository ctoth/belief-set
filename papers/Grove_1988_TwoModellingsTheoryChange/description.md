Grove gives two formal modellings for AGM-style theory change: systems of spheres over maximal consistent extensions and sentence-ordering relations.
The paper proves representation theorems connecting those models to Gardenfors revision postulates and to partial-meet contraction.
For this project it provides the clearest model-theoretic target for implementing AGM revision over finite worlds, spheres, or total preorders.
