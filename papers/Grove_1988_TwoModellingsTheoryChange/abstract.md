## Our Interpretation

Grove proposes two concrete representation models for AGM-style theory change: systems of spheres centered on the worlds compatible with a theory, and sentence-ordering relations that determine which propositions survive revision by a new sentence. The paper proves that these models capture exactly the Gardenfors revision postulates considered and explains how they are dual to the partial-meet contraction representation.
