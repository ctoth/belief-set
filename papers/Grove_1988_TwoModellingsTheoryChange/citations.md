## Key Citations for Follow-up

- Alchourron, Gardenfors, and Makinson 1985: the partial-meet contraction and revision-function representation that Grove connects to sphere semantics.
- Lewis 1973: the source of system-of-spheres semantics adapted here from counterfactuals to theory change.
- Gardenfors 1986: conditionals and belief revision context, including the monotonicity issue Grove discusses.
- Segerberg, "On the logic of small changes to theories I": cited as the prompt for requiring plausible and natural models of small theory changes.
